Dirty Sock - Demo 2025

About

Dirty Sock - Demo 2025 was created as a playful project by Jude, meant as a fake "band demo" recorded purely for fun. The idea was to embrace the raw, chaotic energy of a fictional band while taking the piss in a lighthearted way. The album was recorded in early 2025 and released physically on CD as Demo 2025 on March 15, 2025.

Later, the project saw a reworked streaming release under a new name: Shhh! It's the Dirty Sock band! The change was due to streaming platform guidelines that didn’t allow the use of "Demo 2025" in the title. As a joke, Jude decided to parody the Never Mind The Bollocks, Here's The Sex Pistols album cover, resulting in the new name and artwork.

The streaming version also features some tracklist changes—MOUNTAIN TOP PRICK and My Hero Billy were removed and replaced with two entirely new songs with vocals. The original tracks were instrumental, intentionally loud, heavily clipped, and served no real purpose other than noise. Jude felt that they might become boring in a streaming context, leading to their replacement with brand new songs.

Release Information

Streaming Release (as "Shhh! It's the Dirty Sock band!"): April 4, 2025 (Different track order with some tracks replaced by unreleased ones)
